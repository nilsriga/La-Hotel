Sunny Comfort

Welcome to Gibson Royal, your ultimate destination for sunny comfort in the heart of Los Angeles, Hawthorne. Nestled in a vibrant neighborhood, our hotel offers a perfect blend of luxury and relaxation. Enjoy the warm California sun by our sparkling pool, or unwind in our lush garden oasis.

At Gibson Royal, we pride ourselves on providing exceptional service and a memorable stay. Our friendly staff is dedicated to ensuring your experience is extraordinary. Discover the best of Los Angeles with our convenient location, just minutes away from iconic attractions and entertainment. Come and experience the sunny comfort that awaits you at Gibson Royal.

Rooms

Our cozy rooms at Gibson Royal offer all the essentials for a comfortable stay, including free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs. Each room is designed with simplicity and functionality in mind, ensuring a pleasant experience for our guests.

Restaurant

Our on-site restaurant at Gibson Royal serves a variety of dishes, including local favorites like California-style tacos and international cuisine such as Italian pasta. Open daily from 7 AM to 10 PM, it offers both indoor and outdoor seating options.

Spa & Wellness

Our Spa and Wellness center at Gibson Royal offers a serene escape in the heart of the city. Relax and rejuvenate in our outdoor pool, surrounded by lush greenery and comfortable lounge chairs. Whether you're looking to unwind after a long day or simply enjoy some leisure time, our spa facilities provide the perfect setting for relaxation and well-being.
